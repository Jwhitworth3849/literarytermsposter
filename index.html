Symbolism

My favorite example of symbolism is in the book “Touching Spirit Bear” by Ben Mikaelsen. In this story the most obvious choice for symbolism would be the spirit bear itself, but I have a hard time connecting the spirit bear with Cole’s personality. Instead I’m choosing fire, to symbolize Cole's anger. The quote “Cole stared suddenly into the fire, then let his gaze wander. He had wanted revenge but felt little joy from this act.” shows this very well.
